Tulips



Scientific Name:

Tulipa

Origins:

The Tulip are originally from Central Asia where Turkey was the first country cultivating its seeds. Then they were introduce in Western Europe in the late 16th century by Carolus Clusius.

Before the 17th century the tulips was used for medicinal purposes after that century gained it was most common used for decoration making them really popular.

Meaning:

Across time Tulips had been loved by the people and getting stuck with the meaning of deep love and perfect.

Tips and Growing Process

The first step to grow Tulips is to place the seed in a hole 6-8 inches deep in a pot or the ground to a full sun area and a good drainage, (if you are potting it, the pot better have hole on the bottom) and keep the soil moist. Tulips normally bloom between March and May
