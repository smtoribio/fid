Golden Barrel Captus



Scientific Name:

Echinocactus Grusonii

Origins:

The Golden Barrel Captus is endemic to east-central Mexico, and there are 30 species of this type species found in the dessert of southwester United States and northern of Mexico.

Meaning:

In Native American culture because the cacti can survive on hard condition, they are the symbols of warmth and unconditional, enduring love.

Tips and Growing Process

If you are potting a barrel keep in a warm room and in a sunny location, and watered once a week

Tip: Fertilize the captus once a year when it leaves and begins growing again.
