Marigold



Scientific Name:

Tagetes

Origins:

African and French Marigold are indigenous to Mexico, Guatemala, tropical America, and South America. Fun fact, Marigolds are known as the flower of the dead in pre-Hispanic Mexico on the celebration of the dead know as Dia de Muertos.

Meaning:

Marigold represent power, strength, and light that lives inside of a person.

Tips and Growing Process

Marigold are easy to grow flowers just place them on the soil or in a pot when the frost season is over, waterd regulary or keep the soil moist, also place them in a spot with full sunlight. Their blooming time is from spring until the first frost.

Tip: Applaing fertilizerwhile they are growing can give the Marigold a boost for their growth and lasting longer flowers.
