Aloe Vera



Scientific Name:

Aloe Vera

Origins:

The Aloe Vera is Native from Oman in West-Asia, and then introduce to the rest of the world.

Before the 17th century the tulips was used for medicinal purposes after that century gained it was most common used for decoration making them really popular.

Meaning:

Aloe Vera represents good fortune, health, and beauty.

Tips and Growing Process

Taking care of a Aloe Vera plant has no science since they are low maintenance. You do not need to water it regularly because is a dry plant but keep in mind to not overwatered and not give them enough light which will affect the condition of your plant.
