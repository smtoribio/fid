Saguaro Captus



Scientific Name:

Carnegiea gigantea

Origins:

Saguaro is native to the Sonoran Desert in Arizona, and its bloossom is the state wilflower in Arizona. the captus bloom last weeks of May throught the first week in June.

Meaning:

Saguaros expresses the idea of standing tall, adapting to the environment, and providing shelter to other.

Tips and Growing Process

Saguaro grow well in dry soil and with low leves of water.
